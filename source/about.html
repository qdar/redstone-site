About us

Redstone Tech Solutions having primarily top-notch IT-professionals assures our clients’ cyber resilience at all levels and all dimensions what is achieved by all-embracing systematic approach and implementation of the best cyber security and IT solutions and services.

Our solutions based on best technologies, solid expertise and experience in large-scale penetration testing, risk assessment and cybercrime forensics - we DO NOT offer labels – we DO offer the most efficient solutions for assessing client security and the most robust protection solutions to ensure our clients’ cyber resilience and business continuity.

Starting from 2010 our clients are TOP banks, telecom providers, industrial and mining companies in various countries.

Geographical aspect and specifics are always considered in our projects which is why our solutions portfolio and services are not limited to global IT and cyber security solutions – we have deep and solid expertise in extremely competitive Russian solutions what gives us tremendous flexibility and independence.
