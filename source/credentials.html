Credentials

Most of our clients become regulars after the first project, which is a vivid proof of our professionalism and result-oriented approach. Here are just a few of our credentials; to date, we have completed more than two hundred large contracts, most of which, started with a small vulnerability testing or a customer's wish to

implement particular solution and turned with the time into a step-by-step program with clear priorities, project justifications and a roadmap for their implementation depending on the current threats, aimed to improve cyber security level as such

  • Novikombank

    • Rostech's bank and subsidiary
    • 10 000 users
    Banking

    Pain points

    Arrange top class complex cyber security, launch SIEM system - the client was exercising multiple seriously prepared attacks especially after Feb 2022 and needed to exchange incident information with regionally distributed subsidiaries of the mother company

    Approach

    All embracing phased cyber security project + SIEM launch:

    1. Infrastructure & network audit
    2. Network redesign, firewalls’ rules change and further daily track and update
    3. Hidden cyber security policies’ audit performed
    4. Cyber security business process audit
    5. Cyber security support with 30 min incident reaction and 2 hours cyber security incident resolution SLA
    6. NAD (Network Attack Discovery)
    7. Sandbox implementation
    8. Web Application Firewall implementation
    9. Honey pots implemented to deceive hackers
    10. HW DDOS attacks prevention solution rolled out on premise with impressive resistance speed of up to 4 Gigabit/sec
    11. Our own virtualization farm in used in favor of the Client to emulate Client’s infrastructure
    12. SIEM system implemented

    Execution time

    24 months overall*, 12 months to roll out protection for critical processes and infrastructure *extended lead time due to client’s complicated internal approval processes

    Solutions applied

    1. Next Generation Firewalls
    2. Sandboxes
    3. Web Application Firewalls
    4. Honey Pots
    5. NAD (Network Attack Discovery)
    6. Mitigator anti DDOS
    7. Cyber Security Support service
