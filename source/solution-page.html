Firewalls

To be set-up between Internet and internal networks. Firewall set-up is essentially important! Firewall is nearly useless without it. It’s a consulting job we have deepest expertise in with all vendors.

Solutions & Services

  • Active Directory protection (Domain controller)

    Even if you are not a big company yet we strongly recommend to think of having Active Directory arranged as a digital directory that keeps track of who has access to what.

  • Antiviruses and anti-APT

    (Advance Persistent Threat) – EDR (End Point Detection & Response) – XDR (Extended Detection & Response)

    Can be launched decentralized - per user or can have centralized management - to be used per server

    These helps to protect both end-points and servers, to resist malware, ransomware and APT threats

  • Sandbox

    This type of protection is a complete emulation of the work station - all traffic is transferred through the sandbox, if contamination happens the sandbox collapses
